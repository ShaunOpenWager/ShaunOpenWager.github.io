Coming Soon! Our first Augmented Reality Game

We're all super excited to be working on a new, Augmented Reality ("AR") title, expected to launch in the Fall of 2017. The new game will feature real-world interaction using your phone; battles, puzzles and collection quests that provide you with endless paths of adventure while you travel around in the real world. Additionally, we have a secondary game built in, which allows you to keep progressing even when you can't be outside! The world-inside-a-world lets you battle, collect, build and solve while sitting at home, on an airplane or laying in bed.
